# What is Mindset?

![What is Mindset](/images/mindset.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
This is where everything starts 🧠🔥
</p>

<p>
Your mindset is the way you think about yourself and the world.
</p>

<p>
It controls:
</p>

- Your decisions
- Your actions
- Your reactions
- Your results

<p>
Change your mindset…
and you change your life.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Why Mindset Matters

<p>
Two people can have the same skills…
but different results.
</p>

<p>
The difference?
<strong>How they think.</strong>
</p>

<p>
Your mindset decides:
</p>

- if you try or quit
- if you grow or stay stuck

<div class="line"></div>

## Types of Mindset

- Positive vs Negative mindset
- Growth vs Fixed mindset

<p>
A fixed mindset avoids challenges.
</p>

<p>
A growth mindset learns from them.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Truth

<p>
You are not your past.
</p>

<p>
You are not your thoughts.
</p>

<p>
You can change your mindset…
and become a different person.
</p>

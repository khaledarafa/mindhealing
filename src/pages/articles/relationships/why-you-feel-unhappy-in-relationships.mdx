# Why Do Relationships Feel So Emotionally Exhausting?

![Why Do Relationships Feel So Emotionally Exhausting?](/images/relationships.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
Some people are not entering relationships for love anymore… they are entering them for emotional rescue 💀
</p>

<p>
People used to look for a life partner.
</p>

<p>
Now many people enter relationships
like they are hiring an entire rescue team 😂
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## What People Expect Today

<p>
The other person is expected to be:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Batman during fear</li>
<li>a bank during financial stress</li>
<li>a therapist during emotional breakdowns</li>
<li>a bodyguard against anxiety</li>
<li>a chef when life feels heavy</li>
<li>a poet during sadness</li>
<li>and an Uber driver for every emotional emergency 😂</li>
</ul>

<p>
And of course they also have to be:
</p>

<ul>
<li>kind all the time</li>
<li>understanding all the time</li>
<li>available all the time</li>
<li>romantic all the time</li>
<li>and somehow wise enough to solve every problem 💀</li>
</ul>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Problem Is Not Emotional Needs

<p>
Every human being wants comfort.
</p>

<p>
Wants emotional safety.
</p>

<p>
Wants to feel supported.
</p>

<p>
The problem begins…
when the other person stops being a partner…
</p>

<p>
and becomes a full-time emotional support system.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Why Are People Becoming Like This?

<p>
Because many people enter relationships
already emotionally exhausted inside.
</p>

<p>
Terrified of loneliness.
</p>

<p>
Feeling “not enough.”
</p>

<p>
Unable to sit peacefully with themselves.
</p>

<p>
So they look for someone
to carry all of that pain for them.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Problem Is That Humans Have Limits

<p>
At first…
people try.
</p>

<p>
They care.
</p>

<p>
They comfort.
</p>

<p>
They give attention.
</p>

<p>
But after a while…
</p>

<p>
they start collapsing under the pressure.
</p>

<p>
Because they realize
they are not being asked for love…
</p>

<p>
they are being asked for emotional salvation.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## That Is Why Relationships Become Suffocating

<p>
One person says:
</p>

<p>
“I don’t feel emotionally safe.”
</p>

<p>
Even though the other person is trying their best.
</p>

<p>
Another says:
</p>

<p>
“She’s never satisfied.”
</p>

<p>
Even though she may simply be carrying old fears and emotional wounds.
</p>

<p>
In the end…
both people slowly begin resenting each other.
</p>

<p>
When sometimes the truth is:
</p>

<p>
neither person is evil.
</p>

<p>
They were simply expecting from each other
more than humans can realistically give.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Social Media Made It Worse

<p>
Every day people see:
</p>

<ul>
<li>flowers</li>
<li>travel</li>
<li>gifts</li>
<li>surprises</li>
<li>perfect-looking relationships</li>
</ul>

<p>
So normal love starts looking boring.
</p>

<p>
And every small mistake
starts feeling like a disaster.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Painful Truth

<p>
Sometimes the problem is not that the other person does not love you.
</p>

<p>
Sometimes the problem is expecting them
to provide things no human can provide constantly.
</p>

<p>
That is why some people
move from relationship to relationship…
</p>

<p>
yet keep leaving with the same disappointment every time.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Before You Go...

<p>
I know part of you
might feel uncomfortable reading this.
</p>

<p>
Especially if you grew up believing
real love should “heal everything.”
</p>

<p>
But the truth is:
humans were never designed
to carry each other completely.
</p>

<p>
A healthy relationship is not about finding someone
who erases every fear inside you.
</p>

<p>
It is about finding someone
who walks beside you…
while you are finally learning to face yourself.
</p>

<p>
Continue to the next article…
</p>

<p>
because you are about to understand
why loneliness itself
makes people fall in love with the wrong person 💀
</p>

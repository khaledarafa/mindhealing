# Negative Thinking

![Negative Thinking](/images/mindset.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
This is what drains your energy every day ⚡
</p>

<p>
Negative thinking is automatic.
</p>

<p>
It happens without you noticing.
</p>

<p>
And over time…
it shapes your entire reality.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Why You Think Negatively

- Your brain tries to protect you
- You focus on problems more than solutions
- You repeat old patterns
- You expect the worst

<p>
It feels real…
but it is just a habit.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## How Negative Thinking Works

<p>
One negative thought leads to another.
</p>

<p>
Then it becomes a loop:
</p>

- Thought → Emotion → Action → Result

<p>
If the thought is negative…
everything after it becomes negative.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## How to Stop Negative Thinking

### 1. Notice the Thought

<p>
Awareness is the first step.
</p>

<p>
Catch the thought early.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

### 2. Don’t Believe Everything

<p>
Not every thought is true.
</p>

<p>
Question it:
“Is this real… or just fear?”
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

### 3. Shift Your Focus

<p>
Your mind follows your focus.
</p>

<p>
Move your attention to:
</p>

- Solutions
- Actions
- Progress

<div class="line"></div>

### 4. Take Action

<p>
Thinking stops when action starts.
</p>

<p>
Do something…
even if it’s small.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Truth

<p>
You are not your thoughts.
</p>

<p>
You are the one observing them.
</p>

<p>
Control your thinking…
and you control your life.
</p>

# What Are Affirmations?
![What Are Affirmations](/images/affirmations.jpg)
<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
This might change how you talk to yourself 🧠🔥

<p>
Every day… you are talking to yourself.
</p>

<p>
Not out loud…
but inside your head.
</p>

<p>
And those thoughts…
are shaping your life.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## What Are Affirmations?

<p>
Affirmations are simple statements you repeat to yourself.
</p>

<p>
But they are not just words…
</p>

<p>
They are instructions to your mind.
</p>

- “I am confident”
- “I deserve success”
- “I am improving every day”

<p>
The more you repeat them…
the more your brain starts to believe them.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Why Affirmations Work

<p>
Your brain learns through repetition.
</p>

<p>
If you repeat negative thoughts…
you build a negative mindset.
</p>

<p>
If you repeat positive thoughts…
you build a stronger mindset.
</p>

<p>
Your mind doesn’t know the difference between
what is real…
and what is repeated often.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Problem Most People Have

<p>
They say affirmations…
but don’t feel anything.
</p>

<p>
Or they stop after a few days.
</p>

<p>
And then they say:
“It doesn’t work.”
</p>

<p>
But the problem is not the affirmation…
it’s the lack of repetition.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## What Affirmations Actually Do

- Change your inner voice
- Build self-confidence
- Reduce negative thinking
- Help you take action

<p>
They don’t magically change your life…
</p>

<p>
They change you.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Truth

<p>
You are already using affirmations…
</p>

<p>
But most of them are negative.
</p>

<p>
Change what you repeat…
and your life will start to change.
</p>

# How to Build Self Worth

![How to Build Self Worth](/images/self-worth.jpg)

<p>
Your self worth controls how you see yourself…
and how you allow others to treat you.
</p>

<p>
If you feel like you are not enough,
you will doubt yourself,
settle for less,
and avoid opportunities.
</p>

<p>
But here’s the truth:
</p>

<p>
<strong>You are not born with high or low self worth.</strong>
</p>

<p>
You build it.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Why You Feel Low Self Worth

<ul>
<li>You compare yourself to others</li>
<li>You depend on validation</li>
<li>You focus on your mistakes</li>
<li>You ignore your progress</li>
</ul>

<p>
Over time…
these patterns shape how you see yourself.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Step 1: Stop Comparing Yourself

<p>
Comparison destroys your self worth.
</p>

<p>
There will always be someone better in something.
</p>

<p>
Focus on your own growth instead.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Step 2: Change Your Inner Voice

<p>
The way you talk to yourself matters.
</p>

<ul>
<li>“I’m not enough” → “I am improving”</li>
<li>“I always fail” → “I am learning”</li>
</ul>

<p>
This slowly rebuilds your self image.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Step 3: Keep Promises to Yourself

<p>
Every promise you keep…
builds trust with yourself.
</p>

<p>
Start small and stay consistent.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Step 4: Accept Yourself as You Are

<p>
You don’t need to be perfect to be valuable.
</p>

<p>
Accept your flaws…
and keep improving at the same time.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Step 5: Use Affirmations Daily

<p>
Your thoughts shape your self worth.
</p>

<p>
Repeat daily:
</p>

<ul>
<li>I am enough</li>
<li>I deserve good things</li>
<li>I value myself</li>
</ul>

<p>
You can also check this:
</p>

<a href="/articles/affirmations/daily-affirmations-for-confidence.html">
Daily Affirmations →
</a>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Truth

<p>
You don’t need to become someone else
to feel worthy.
</p>

<p>
You just need to start treating yourself differently.
</p>

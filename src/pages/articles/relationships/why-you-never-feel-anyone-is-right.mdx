# Why Do You Always Feel Like No One Is Right For You?

![Why Do You Always Feel Like No One Is Right For You?](/images/relationships.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
Maybe the problem is not other people… maybe it’s the emptiness you are trying to escape from 💀
</p>

<p>
A girl says:
</p>

<p>
“Every time I meet someone new…
I feel like he’s not the right person.”
</p>

<p>
So she leaves.
</p>

<p>
Then starts over with someone else.
</p>

<p>
And then someone else.
</p>

<p>
In the end,
she stays convinced that:
</p>

<p>
“I just haven’t found the right person yet.”
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Painful Truth

<p>
Sometimes the problem is not that people are wrong for you.
</p>

<p>
Sometimes you are looking for someone
to heal a wound they never created.
</p>

<p>
What does that mean?
</p>

<p>
It means that many times…
you are not searching for love.
</p>

<p>
You are searching for compensation.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## What Are People Really Looking For?

<p>
Someone who grew up without emotional warmth…
will search for endless emotional reassurance.
</p>

<p>
Someone with an emotionally distant father…
may crave constant attention.
</p>

<p>
Someone who was betrayed before…
may need reassurance every five minutes.
</p>

<p>
Someone who secretly feels “not enough”…
may look for a person who constantly validates them.
</p>

<p>
Someone terrified of loneliness…
may become attached to any small amount of attention.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Real Problem

<p>
The real problem is that many people
keep searching for someone to “complete” them.
</p>

<p>
But a person who enters relationships
feeling deeply incomplete…
</p>

<p>
will never truly feel satisfied.
</p>

<p>
Because they will always need another emotional dose:
</p>

<ul>
<li>attention</li>
<li>validation</li>
<li>reassurance</li>
<li>comfort</li>
<li>proof of love</li>
</ul>

<p>
And the moment those feelings calm down…
</p>

<p>
they start thinking:
</p>

<p>
“Maybe this person is not right for me.”
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Humans Are Not Emotional Rescue Centers

<p>
Some people enter relationships
looking for:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Batman during hard times</li>
<li>a bank during financial stress</li>
<li>a therapist during emotional breakdowns</li>
<li>a bodyguard against fear</li>
<li>and a poet with ancient wisdom 😂</li>
</ul>

<p>
Then they become disappointed
when they discover
the other person is just…
human.
</p>

<p>
They get tired.
</p>

<p>
They make mistakes.
</p>

<p>
They feel overwhelmed.
</p>

<p>
And they cannot fill an emotional void this deep.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Maybe The Problem Is Not Other People

<p>
Sometimes you do not reject people
because they are wrong for you.
</p>

<p>
Sometimes nobody can heal
something you have never faced inside yourself.
</p>

<p>
That is why some people
go through relationship after relationship…
</p>

<p>
yet still carry the same emptiness every time.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Truth

<p>
A healthy relationship is not supposed to make you feel “complete.”
</p>

<p>
It is supposed to help you share your life…
not escape yourself.
</p>

<p>
Because when someone enters a relationship emotionally broken…
</p>

<p>
they usually expect from the other person
more than any human being can realistically give.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Before You Go...

<p>
I know this might feel confusing right now.
</p>

<p>
Maybe for the first time,
you are asking yourself:
</p>

<p>
“Wait…
if this isn’t love,
then what have I been doing all these years?”
</p>

<p>
But be gentle with yourself ❤️
</p>

<p>
You are not crazy.
</p>

<p>
You are not “too much.”
</p>

<p>
And you are not impossible to love.
</p>

<p>
Most of us learned love the wrong way.
</p>

<p>
We grew up believing
another person was supposed to fill the emptiness inside us.
</p>

<p>
Maybe this is the first time
you are seeing yourself clearly.
</p>

<p>
And that is not a bad thing…
that is the beginning of awareness.
</p>

<p>
Continue to the next article…
because it will help you understand
why so many people enter relationships
looking for a superhero to save them 😂
</p>

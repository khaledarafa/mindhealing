# How to Increase Self Worth (Part 1)

![How to Increase Self Worth](/images/self-worth.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
Keep going… you're almost there 🔥
</p>

<p>
Here’s the truth…
</p>

<p>
Self worth doesn’t change by information.
</p>

<p>
It changes through daily practice.
</p>

<p>
Every time you repeat new thoughts and behaviors,
your subconscious slowly replaces the old programming.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## 1. Daily Affirmations

<p>
Your mind is programmed by repetition — especially when emotions are involved.
</p>

<p>
Start your day, or end it, by repeating affirmations out loud.
Even if you don’t believe them at first.
</p>

<p><strong>Examples:</strong></p>

- I allow good things to come to me easily
- I deserve love, money, and success without struggle
- Every day I allow more abundance into my life
- Money flows to me from expected and unexpected sources
- I am enough exactly as I am

<p>
If you record them in your own voice and listen daily,
the effect becomes much stronger.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## 2. Visualization

<p>
Visualization is not imagination…
it is rehearsal for your subconscious mind.
</p>

<p>
Take 5 minutes daily.
Close your eyes and ask yourself:
</p>

<p>
<strong>
“If I was already living my ideal life…
how would I feel? how would I act?”
</strong>
</p>

<p>
See it as real:
</p>

- You are buying what you want
- You are loved and appreciated
- Your income is growing
- Your goals are becoming real

<p>
Each time you do this,
you break the belief that “this is not for me”.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## 3. Receive Without Resistance

<p>
This is one of the strongest indicators of your self worth.
</p>

<p>
When someone compliments you, how do you react?
</p>

- “No, not really…”
- “It’s nothing…”
- “You’re just being nice…”

<p>
This blocks your ability to receive.
</p>

<p>
Instead, simply say:
</p>

<p><strong>“Thank you, I appreciate that.”</strong></p>

<p>
And feel it.
Accept it.
</p>

<p>
The same goes for gifts or opportunities.
Don’t reject them.
Receive them fully.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Real Secret

<p>
People think they need to feel worthy first…
before they act like it.
</p>

<p>
But the truth is the opposite:
</p>

<p>
<strong>
Act like you deserve it…
until you truly feel that you do.
</strong>
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Simple Daily Exercise

<p>
Write these daily:
</p>

- I deserve ______
- I allow ______ into my life
- I am grateful for ______

<p>
Example:
</p>

- I deserve peaceful love
- I allow money to come easily
- I am grateful for my health and energy

<p>
This slowly replaces your old beliefs with new ones.
</p>

# Affirmations for Health and Healing

![Affirmations for Health and Healing](/images/affirmations.jpg)

<p>
Stress,

and negative thinking
can affect your emotional and physical energy.

<p>
Positive affirmations can help create

## Healing and Health Affirmations

<ul>
<li>My body deserves care and respect</li>
<li>I allow healing energy into my life</li>
<li>I choose healthy habits daily</li>
<li>My mind and body are becoming stronger</li>
<li>I release stress and emotional tension</li>

<li>I deserve rest, peace, and healing</li>

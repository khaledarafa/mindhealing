# Confidence Mistakes You Must Avoid

![Confidence Mistakes](/images/confidence.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
This might change how you see yourself 👀
</p>

<p>
Most people don’t fail because they don’t try.
</p>

<p>
They fail because they are building confidence the wrong way.
</p>

<p>
And they repeat the same mistakes…
again and again.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## 1. Waiting to Feel Ready

<p>
This is the biggest mistake.
</p>

<p>
You think:
“I will start when I feel confident.”
</p>

<p>
But confidence comes after action…
not before it.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## 2. Comparing Yourself to Others

<p>
You see someone ahead of you…
and you feel behind.
</p>

<p>
But you don’t see their journey.
</p>

<p>
Comparison kills confidence fast.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## 3. Seeking Validation

<p>
You wait for people to approve you.
</p>

<p>
You need them to say:
“You’re good.”
</p>

<p>
That means your confidence is not yours.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## 4. Avoiding Discomfort

<p>
You stay in your comfort zone.
</p>

<p>
You avoid situations that make you feel awkward.
</p>

<p>
But confidence grows only in discomfort.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## 5. Talking Bad About Yourself

<p>
“I’m not good enough.”
“I always fail.”
</p>

<p>
Your brain listens…
and starts believing it.
</p>

<p>
Your words shape your identity.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Truth

<p>
Confidence is not about doing everything right.
</p>

<p>
It’s about stopping what is destroying you.
</p>

<p>
Fix the mistakes…
and confidence will grow naturally.
</p>

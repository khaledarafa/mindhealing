# Discover Your Self Worth Level

![Discover Your Self Worth Level](/images/self-worth.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
Keep going… you're almost there 🔥
</p>

<p>
Before you try to improve your life, you need to understand where you currently stand.
</p>

<p>
Your reality today is a reflection of how you see yourself deep inside.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Are You Living in Abundance or Lack?

<p>
Look at your life honestly.
Your emotions, your reactions, and your daily experiences reveal your true self worth level.
</p>

### Ask Yourself:

- Do you feel like you always have to struggle for money?
- Do good opportunities come but quickly disappear?
- Do you feel uncomfortable when someone praises you?
- Do you constantly try to prove your value to others?
- Do you attract stressful or draining relationships?
- Do you fear losing anything good in your life?

<p>
If most of your answers are “yes”, then you may be operating from a low self worth state.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Signs of High Self Worth

- You feel calm and stable inside
- You trust that good things will come
- You don’t feel the need to prove yourself
- You attract better opportunities naturally
- You respect your time, energy, and boundaries

<p>
People with high self worth don’t chase life…
Life comes to them.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Truth You Need to Accept

<p>
Your current results are not random.
</p>

<p>
They are a direct reflection of what you believe you deserve.
</p>

<p>
And the good news is:
You can change this.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Start Becoming Aware

<p>
Don’t judge yourself.
Just observe.
</p>

<p>
Awareness is the first step to change.
</p>

<p>
Once you see clearly where you are,
you can start moving to a better level.
</p>

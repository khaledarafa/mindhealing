# Do You Love The Person… Or The Feeling They Give You?

![Do You Love The Person… Or The Feeling They Give You?](/images/relationships.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
Sometimes we do not fall in love with people… we fall in love with the feeling they gave us 💀
</p>

<p>
A woman meets a man who is:
</p>

<ul>
<li>attentive</li>
<li>kind</li>
<li>emotionally present</li>
<li>comforting</li>
<li>makes her feel important</li>
</ul>

<p>
And suddenly she says:
</p>

<p>
“This is the love of my life.”
</p>

<p>
But sometimes…
she is not really seeing him clearly.
</p>

<p>
She is seeing the thing
she has been missing her entire life.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## People Do Not Always Fall In Love With The Person

<p>
A lot of the time…
we become attached to the feeling.
</p>

<p>
Not the actual person.
</p>

<p>
Someone who grew up emotionally deprived…
</p>

<p>
may instantly fall for anyone
who finally makes them feel emotionally safe.
</p>

<p>
Someone who secretly feels “not enough”…
</p>

<p>
may become deeply attached
to anyone who makes them feel valuable.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## That Is Why The Beginning Feels So Intense

<p>
At the beginning…
people often see each other
through the lens of emotional need.
</p>

<p>
Not through awareness.
</p>

<p>
They are not seeing the full reality.
</p>

<p>
They are seeing:
</p>

<ul>
<li>the safety they crave</li>
<li>the attention they missed</li>
<li>the emotional warmth they lacked</li>
<li>the validation they desperately needed</li>
</ul>

<p>
And that is why the beginning
feels full of obsession and emotional intensity.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Then The Shock Begins

<p>
The problem is…
that feeling cannot stay at the same intensity forever.
</p>

<p>
Life calms down.
</p>

<p>
People get tired.
</p>

<p>
And no normal human being
can keep giving the same emotional high forever.
</p>

<p>
That is when the mind starts saying:
</p>

<p>
“You changed.”
</p>

<p>
“You are not the same anymore.”
</p>

<p>
Even though sometimes…
the person did not actually change at all.
</p>

<p>
You simply started seeing them
without the filter of emotional need and fantasy.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Hidden Thing Most People Never Notice

<p>
People watch movies and romantic shows…
</p>

<p>
and become obsessed with the beautiful image of relationships.
</p>

<p>
The romance.
</p>

<p>
The emotional intensity.
</p>

<p>
The attention.
</p>

<p>
The feeling of “finally finding your other half.”
</p>

<p>
But very few people notice
the hidden emotional dynamic underneath it all.
</p>

<p>
That both people often entered the relationship
already missing something inside themselves.
</p>

<p>
And they secretly expected
the other person to fill that emptiness.
</p>

<p>
Then once they realize
the other person cannot keep fulfilling that emotional need forever…
</p>

<p>
the problems begin.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Painful Truth

<p>
Sometimes the problem is not that the other person does not love you.
</p>

<p>
The problem is expecting them
to constantly provide things
that no human being can provide forever.
</p>

<p>
That is why many people
move from relationship to relationship…
</p>

<p>
yet keep leaving with the same disappointment every time.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## So What Should You Do?

<p>
Before entering any relationship…
</p>

<p>
ask yourself honestly:
</p>

<ul>
<li>What am I truly looking for?</li>
<li>What emotional need am I trying to fill?</li>
<li>Do I love the person… or the feeling they give me?</li>
<li>If that emotional need disappeared… would I still love this person?</li>
</ul>

<p>
The more someone understands themselves…
</p>

<p>
the more consciously they choose people.
</p>

<p>
Instead of choosing through blind emotional need.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Before You Go...

<p>
I know this might feel uncomfortable to read.
</p>

<p>
Especially if you are realizing
that a big part of your attachment…
</p>

<p>
was not truly about the person,
but about the feeling they gave you.
</p>

<p>
But that does not make you fake.
</p>

<p>
And it does not mean your emotions were a lie.
</p>

<p>
It simply means
there was an old emotional need inside you…
</p>

<p>
and maybe this is the first time
you are finally seeing it clearly.
</p>

<p>
And that is an important beginning.
</p>

<p>
Because once a person truly understands themselves…
</p>

<p>
they stop chasing temporary emotional highs,
and start seeing people more clearly.
</p>

</p>

<p>
because you are about to understand
why loneliness itself
can make people choose the wrong person from the start 💀
</p>

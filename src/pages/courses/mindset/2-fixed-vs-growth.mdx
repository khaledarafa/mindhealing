# Fixed vs Growth Mindset

![Fixed vs Growth Mindset](/images/mindset.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
This will change how you see challenges 🔥
</p>

<p>
There are two main ways people think:
</p>

- Fixed mindset
- Growth mindset

<p>
The difference between them…
changes everything.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## What is a Fixed Mindset?

<p>
A fixed mindset believes:
</p>

- “I am either good or bad at something”
- “I can't change much”
- “If I fail, it means I’m not good enough”

<p>
So people avoid challenges…
to protect their image.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## What is a Growth Mindset?

<p>
A growth mindset believes:
</p>

- “I can improve with effort”
- “Mistakes are part of learning”
- “Failure helps me grow”

<p>
So people take action…
even when it’s hard.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Key Difference

<p>
Fixed mindset says:
<strong>“This is who I am.”</strong>
</p>

<p>
Growth mindset says:
<strong>“This is who I’m becoming.”</strong>
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Why This Matters

<p>
Your mindset decides:
</p>

- If you try or quit
- If you learn or stay stuck
- If you grow or stay the same

<p>
Same situation…
different mindset…
completely different result.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Truth

<p>
You are not limited.
</p>

<p>
You are just thinking in a limited way.
</p>

<p>
Change your mindset…
and your reality will follow.
</p>

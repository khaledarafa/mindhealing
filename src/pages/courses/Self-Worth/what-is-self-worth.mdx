# What is Self Worth?

![What is Self Worth](/images/self-worth.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
Keep going… you're almost there 🔥
</p>

<p>
Have you ever noticed that some people seem to have everything flowing easily in their lives?
Opportunities, money, relationships… everything just works.
</p>

<p>
And others, no matter how hard they try, feel like life is always against them.
</p>

<p>
The difference is not intelligence, effort, or luck.
</p>

<p>
The real difference is something deeper:
<strong>Self Worth.</strong>
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## What Does Self Worth Mean?

<p>
Self worth is your internal feeling about yourself.
</p>

<p>
It is the belief that:
</p>

- You deserve good things without proving yourself
- You deserve love just because you exist
- You deserve success without suffering
- You deserve abundance even if no one around you has it

<p>
It’s the inner voice that says:
</p>

<p>
<strong>"I am enough. I deserve a good life without pain."</strong>
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## What Happens When Self Worth Is Low?

- You feel guilty when something good happens
- You reject compliments
- You overwork to prove your value
- You sabotage good opportunities

<p>
Not because you are weak…
But because your mind is programmed to believe you don’t deserve yet.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## What Happens When Self Worth Is High?

- You receive good things easily
- You stop chasing validation
- You attract better opportunities
- You feel calm and confident

<p>
Life becomes easier… not because it changed,
but because you did.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Truth

<p>
Self worth is not something you are born with.
It is something you build.
</p>

<p>
Every thought, every action, every belief…
either raises it or destroys it.
</p>

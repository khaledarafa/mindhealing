# How to Stop Overthinking

![How to Stop Overthinking](/images/confidence.jpg)

<div id="progress"></div>

<p style="font-size:12px; opacity:0.6;">
This is where everything changes 🔥
</p>

<p>
Overthinking is not intelligence.
</p>

<p>
It feels like you are solving problems…
but in reality, you are just delaying action.
</p>

<p>
The more you think…
the less you move.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## Why You Overthink

- You want everything to be perfect
- You are afraid of making mistakes
- You care too much about what people think
- You don’t trust yourself yet

<p>
Your brain is trying to protect you…
but it is also trapping you.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## The Problem

<p>
Overthinking creates fake problems.
</p>

<p>
You imagine:
</p>

- What if I fail?
- What if they judge me?
- What if I’m not good enough?

<p>
Most of these things never happen.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

## How to Stop Overthinking

### 1. Take Action Immediately

<p>
The moment you notice overthinking…
do something.
</p>

<p>
Even a small action breaks the loop.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

### 2. Set a Time Limit

<p>
Give yourself 5 minutes to think.
</p>

<p>
After that…
you must act.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

### 3. Accept Imperfection

<p>
Done is better than perfect.
</p>

<p>
Progress beats overthinking every time.
</p>

<div class="line"></div>

### 4. Move Your Body

<p>
Overthinking happens in your head.
</p>

<p>
Movement brings you back to reality.
</p>

- Walk
- Exercise
- Do anything physical

<div class="line"></div>

## The Truth

<p>
You don’t need more thinking.
</p>

<p>
You need more action.
</p>

<p>
Confidence grows when you move…
not when you think.
</p>
